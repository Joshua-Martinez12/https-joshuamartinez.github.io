Poems The Ones who came first Sankofa

Class of 1968

Celebrating the achievements, courage, and impact of Black leaders who shaped history. The stories and culture of Black People have been muffled and surpressed in favor of a eurocentric lens of history. The aim for this page is to highlight all the people in our shared history and culture bringing all the great ones together from across the internet and history into one stop to spread our and your Black Consciousness. Maria and Vivian acknowledge there Black Consciousness by going back and getting it from their ancestral spirits. Joshua acknowledges his Black Consiciousness by paying respects to his environment and where he comes from.
