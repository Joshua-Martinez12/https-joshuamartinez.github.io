Enough to break the chains” Love feels dangerous in a world like this, But still my heart reaches for yours. Even bruised, it keeps beating. I’ve been broken before. I’ve seen the world turn cold And pretend my pain was invisible. But still my heart keeps beating like It refuses to die. I believe in small miracles: Your hand in mine, A breath i didnt think i had left a love that survives the wreckage. Love scare the world Because it can't be chained, Because it heals what was Meant to stay wounded. But we love anyway. We hope anyway. We rise anyway. Hope grows in us Like a secret revolution. A quiet promise That we deserve more than survival. And together even Shaking, even scared We are brave enough To break the bars they built around our lives.
